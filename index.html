Paulo Carvão

Paulo Carvão is a Senior Fellow at Harvard Kennedy School's Mossavar-Rahmani Center for Business and Government, where he focuses on Tech Policy and AI regulation. A former IBM executive with over 30 years of experience in technology and business, he currently advises tech startups, invests in venture capital, and serves as the inaugural Entrepreneur-in-Residence at Harvard Kennedy School's GovLab. He writes for Forbes, Harvard Business Review, Fast Company, The Conversation, Lawfare, The Hill, and other publications.
